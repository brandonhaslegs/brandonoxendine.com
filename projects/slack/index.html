Slack

This Slack branding concept was done while Slack was in the process of considering revitalising their brand. In addition to exploring things closer to their existing brand, I designed this completely new concept.

Role: Concept, Design, Lettering; Made at Fuzzco

The stretchy rainbow-colored band represented working on a team together in perfect cohesion. The logo could be seen in any combination of stretchiness, just like Slack can be used in any combination of organizational structures.

I designed a full lowercase alphabet to be used sparingly in advertising and swag materials.
